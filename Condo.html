Condo Insurance


Owning a condo comes with unique coverage needs that differ from renters or single-family homeowners. At Sunrise Texas Insurance Agency, we offer specialized protection to help safeguard what your condo association’s policy may not.

What Does Condo Insurance Cover?
  • Personal Property: Covers your belongings — furniture, electronics, clothing, and more — in case of fire, theft, or other covered losses. Most condo association policies do not protect your personal items.
  • Improvements & Upgrades: Protection for custom features you've added, such as cabinets, flooring, lighting, and built-in fixtures your association’s policy may exclude.
  • Loss Assessment: Helps pay your share if the condo association issues a special assessment for repairs to shared areas due to a covered event.
  • Additional Living Expenses: Covers temporary living costs if your unit becomes uninhabitable after a covered loss.
  • Personal Liability: Protection if someone is injured or their property is damaged and you're held legally responsible.
  • Medical Payments to Others: Pays medical bills for guests injured in your condo, regardless of fault.
  • Rented Condo Coverage: Provides liability and personal property coverage if you rent out your condo.
