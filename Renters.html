Renters Insurance

Even if you don't own the property you live in, your belongings and personal liability still need protection. At Sunrise Texas Insurance Agency, we offer renter-friendly coverage that helps you recover from the unexpected.


Why Renters Need Insurance:

Imagine a fire damages your furniture — or a guest is injured in your home. Renters insurance helps cover the cost to replace personal items, manage medical expenses, and protect you from potential legal claims.


  • Personal Property
    Protection for your belongings — like furniture, electronics, and clothing — in the event of fire, theft, or other covered losses.

  • Personal Liability
    Covers bodily injury or property damage to others caused by a covered incident.

  • Medical Payments to Others
    Helps pay medical expenses if a guest is injured in your rented home or apartment.

  • Liability Lawsuit Protection
    Provides legal coverage if you're sued for injuries or damages you’re responsible for.
